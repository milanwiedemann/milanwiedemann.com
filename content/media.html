I work as a part-time research assistant for the Oxford Centre for Anxiety Disorders and Trauma (OxCADAT) to create videos for online therapies. I also enjoy filming and editing videos to share my research or other exciting projects.

Video abstracts

My colleague Urška Košir and I systematically reviewed the literature to find out why some adolescent cancer survivors have a harder time adapting to life after illness, see here. We filmed a short video summarising our findings, see our video below :movie_camera:

Videos for psychological online treatments

I’ve mainly worked on creating videos for a newly developed online therapy for PTSD. This video gives a short overview of the internet program, a pilot paper desrcibing the treatment is available here.

Videos for training psychological therapists

I’m also involved in filming and editing videos to help therapists treat clients using specialised cognitive therapies for Social Anxiety Disorder and Posttraumatic Stress Disorder. To see the videos and other resources go to the OxCADAT Resources website.
